🔱 What is Brahmacharya?›

Brahmacharya is one of the most powerful ancient disciplines from the Vedic tradition. It means conservation and sublimation of your vital energy (Virya/Ojas) for higher spiritual, mental, and physical growth.

"Brahmacharya is the divine power. One who preserves it becomes invincible." — Swami Vivekananda

❓ FAQ›
What if I slip and break my streak? ▼
Do not give up. Use Streak Redemption (500 pts) to recover 1 missed day, or start fresh. Every warrior falls — what matters is rising again.
How does 5AM Club work? ▼
Check in between 4:00–6:00 AM. 4:00–4:59 AM = 60 pts ⭐ Early Bird. 5:00 AM sharp = 60 pts. 5:01–5:59 AM = 60 minus minutes late. Join the live session after attendance.
Does this app work offline? ▼
Yes! BrahmaMode is a PWA. Install it from your browser — it works offline, loads instantly, and has a home screen icon.
